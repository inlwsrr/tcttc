Thom Yorke

Thom Yorke, the frontman of Radiohead, is also a solo artist and DJ known for blending electronic, ambient, and experimental music. His DJ sets often mix IDM, glitch, and eclectic soundscapes. As a solo artist, Yorke has released albums like *The Eraser* and *Anima*, pushing the boundaries between rock and electronic music, heavily influenced by artists like Aphex Twin. He's a key figure in merging these genres, using his DJ work to explore new sonic textures.
